Shawarma Recipe

Easy to make chicken shwarma need needs little bit of technique to get maximum flavour. Served with rice/fries and veggies. White sauce and red sauce for extra PUNCH!

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Garlic
  • Lemon zest and juice
  • Cumin seeds
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cloves
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Mortal and pestle cumin seeds, garlic and cloves.
  2. Mix mayonnaise, lemon zest, juice of lemon and garlic mixture together. Combine thoroughly.
  3. Add salt and pepper.
  4. Cut thighs length wise and combine with marinade. Let set for few hours.
  5. Add olive oil and cook chicken in batches on cast iron, get good browning.
